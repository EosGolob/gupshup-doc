Qus No 1 Can We capture submit attandance with QR code.   
ans  No, it's not possible to capture someone's phone simply by scanning a QR code.
QR codes are static images containing encoded data, typically a URL, text, or other information. 
Scanning a QR code with a smartphone's camera doesn't grant access to the device itself or its data.
However, QR codes can be used to initiate actions on a smartphone, such as opening a website,sending a text message, or initiating a phone call.
